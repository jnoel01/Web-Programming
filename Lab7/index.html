My parents sillily decided to move to Staten Island, which I like to call Shitten Island and I transferred my schooling to Wagner High School which made me super sad. Because I enjoy the arts a lot and I am indeed a stem major (who wants to do more analytics stuff with cs), I try to incorporate art into my code.
